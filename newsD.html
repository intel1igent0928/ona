Juru Internship Program 2025

#News February 3, 2025

Juru is delighted to announce its Paid Internship Program designed for third and fourth-year students/recent graduates with a desire to gain hands-on experience in consultancy and various engineering disciplines. We encourage candidates from the Central Asia region to apply to our Internship Program in the following areas:

  • Renewable Energy (solar, wind) 
  • Electrical Engineer   
  • Energy Efficiency and Audit
  • Power Systems Engineer 
  • SCADA/Automation Engineer 
  • Telecom Engineer                   
  • Instrumentation & Control Engineer 
  • Civil Engineer 
  • HVAC Engineer 
  • Water Supply / Wastewater/ Drainage 
  • Geological/Geotechnical Engineering
  • Hydrometeorology/Hydrology 
  • Architect 
  • Executive Assistant
  • Project Assistant 
  • General Layout 
  • Environmental     
  • Biodiversity 
  • Socio-Economic                        
  • Energy Analyst/ Economist 
  • Low Carbon Analyst                       
  • BD Assistant 
  • HR Assistant                                          
  • Marketing Assistant                                                                                                     

What We Expect From You

  • You are the senior BSc/MSc student or a recent graduate in the field you are applying to the internship;
  • You are highly organized, diligent, and self-motivated;
  • You have a good command of English (particularly written);
  • Your knowledge of Russian and Uzbek can be an asset;
  • You have strong analytical and problem-solving abilities;
  • You have strong verbal and written communication skills;
  • You are a good user of Microsoft Office programs;
  • Previous internship/work experience in a similar area is an advantage.

What We Offer

  • Our internship program helps you to apply your knowledge in real cases by providing hands-on experience;
  • Employment opportunities after the successful completion of the internship;
  • On-the-job training and courses on developing soft skills;
  • Reference Letter after the successful completion of the internship;
  • Excellent working conditions in our office at the center of Tashkent.


Duration of the Internship Program: 3 months

Employment Type: Internship


Selection Process

The selection process is divided into three stages.

  • Stage 1. Submitting the Application Form - in this stage, you should fill out the Google form by submitting your CV.
  • Stage 2. Test/Task Completion - shortlisted candidates from the first stage will need to solve the test/task.
  • Stage 3. Interview - in this stage candidates should go through a face-to-face interview with the hiring manager.


Deadline to submit the application form: March 3, 2025

  • Stage 1 results will be announced by: March 23, 2025
  • Stage 2 results will be announced by: April 7, 2025
  • Final results will be announced by: April 27, 2025
  • Starting date of the Internship: May 1, 2025

*Please note that only the shortlisted candidates will be contacted in every stage, and if you don't hear from us within 3 days after the deadline mentioned above, this means that you are not qualified for the next stage. But you can always apply when the next intake will start.

You can apply through the following link: Juru Internship Program 2025
